1self makes it easy for developers to observe their development activities. Lots of data is collected about developers but bringing all that together in a personalised view is hard at the moment. Developers either have to visit lots of different apps and sites, or write code to push the data into a dashboard. Even worse, some useful data isnâ€™t collected, like local builds. 1self is fixing this by bringing together a vertical platform for the collection, integration and observation of personal development data. Once developers have all this data in one place, they will be able to make and share observations. Whether developers are looking to be more productive, code more healthily or are just interested in their behaviour, we hope that 1self will allow them to discover something new about themselves.

QuantifiedDev is a free open source platform built by developers for developers.

Benefits

QuantifiedDev is Quantified Self for developers - self knowledge through numbers.

Plug in your favourite development tools and other sources of data to create your own private store of development statistics.

Sign up to find out new insights about yourself by discovering new connections between data sets and by comparisons with your peers.

Possibilities

What's the best music for development?
How can I reduce my cycle time?
Is it better to pair or work alone?
How do I compare to my peers?
Which language is most popular?
What effect does build duration have on my focus?

QuantifiedDev gives you these answers and more

Sign up to start exploring

Community

QuantifiedDev is a community project. We need your help to create useful data and to extend the platform.

Once we're up and running QuantifiedDev will be open source. Please help us to extend the capability of QuantifiedDev by building extensions to it that plug into your favourite tools to log the information and deliver the insights that interest you.
