What is 1self?

The Internet of Me

1self is the 1 place for all of the data about you. 1self lets you collect data from all of the websites, apps and things that you interact with. Within 1self, you can explore and visualize the data about you to unlock new insights, tell stories about your activities, understand yourself and your behaviour and compare yourself with your friends, your team and the world.

1self {developers}

Lucky you if you're a developer because the first phase of 1self is all about you! We're building connections and integrations to all of your favourite software development tools so that you can finally have the complete picture of how you do development.

Why use 1self?

For the first time - your data in one place

As a developer, you're used to having your development metrics collected. Never before have they been brought together in one place for your own delectation rather than that of your project manager.

Data -> Information -> Interest -> Actionable Insights

Data is the new oil, so they say. And just like oil, it's pretty useless when it comes out of the ground. In order to be useful it has to be distilled and refined. 1self lets you distill your data to create visualizations that interest you. What's more, you can share the data

Observe yourself. Tell stories.

Do you really know yourself and how you work? With 1self you can make and share observations. Whether you're looking to be more productive, code more healthily or are just found something interesting, 1self will unlock new info about how you develop.

Community

More = Merrier

1self is all about finding answers to big questions and to do that we need big data. By using 1self you can find instant info about yourself but you're also contributing to the community dataset so we can see trends and averages across a large population. You can then compare yourself to the global trends. Your data will be anonymised through these aggregations so don't fear!

Extend the platform

We want 1self to provide instant integration with any tool or dataset that is interesting to you. That's a mighty task so we'd love you to join some of our other community members in extending the platform itself or by building plugins on top of it. Just get stuck in and send us a pull request when you're ready.

Analyse, share and discuss

If you find a new correlation in some data then you can share it with the community and let them see how it affects them. 1self will ensure you get the benefit of the correlations that other community members find. Get together and discuss what the results mean, then explore further.

See your tribe in action in real time

By adding geo data to the events that are logged 1self can plot the action on our realtime globe. See who's talking your language around the world, right now.

Integrations and Plugins

Connect to your favourite development tools

1self works by letting you connect up all of your favorite developement tools. We have built plugins for some of the most popular tools, including IntelliJ Idea, Visual Studio and Sublime Text. We have also built an integration to GitHub.

Simply head to our downloads page to get started with your favourite tools. If your tool isn't there then let us know what you want to track and from which tool or build the plugin/integration yourself.
