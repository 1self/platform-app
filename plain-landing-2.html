Benefits

Know what you measure and act on what you know. QuantifiedDev is Quantified Self for developers. With QuantifiedDev you can plug in your favourite development tools and other sources of data to create your own private store of development statistics. Crowd-sourced analysis. Community powered extensions

Possibilities

What's the best music for development? How can you reduce your cycle time? Is it better for me to pair or work alone? How do I compare to my peers? Which language is most popular? What effect does build duration have on my focus? What hour of the day do you commit most?

Community

QuantifiedDev is a community project. We need your help to create useful data and to extend the platform.

QuantifiedDev is open source under the MIT licence. Get stuck in. Poke around. And please, help us to extend the capability of QuantifiedDev by building extensions to it that plug into your favourite tools and log the information that you're interested in.

Visualisations and analyses of the data within the platform is also open source. We'd like you to help us bring meaning to the data and help to answer the questions that you and many of your peers would like the answers to.

Privacy

We value your privacy. Your data is your data. Identifiable and individual data will always remain private unless you choose to share it. If there are large enough data groups to aggregate your data into without it there being a possibility of identifying you then we will do this so that we can show you comparisons of your data versus the average of a certain group - e.g. How do I compare to other developers around the world?

You can export your data from QuantifiedDev at any time if you want to use it elsewhere. You can also delete your data from QuantifiedDev at any time, though we hope you won't want to.
