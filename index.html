QuantifiedDev is a free open source platform built by developers for developers.

Benefits

QuantifiedDev is Quantified Self for developers - self knowledge through numbers.

Plug in your favourite development tools and other sources of data to create your own private store of development statistics.

Sign up to find out new insights about yourself by discovering new connections between data sets and by comparisons with your peers.

Visual Studio extension

Our VS 2013 and VS 2012 extension currently logs each build that you perform and whether that build succeeded or failed. It will also ask you for your location when you initially install it. Support for 2010 is coming soon. Sign up to be notified when VS2010 support is ready.

No sign up required. Logs anonymously

Download VS 2012 extension
No sign up required. Logs anonymously

Visit your QuantifiedDev dashboard and enter your stream id and token from the VS extension window to see your data.

Privacy

We value your privacy. Your data is your data. Identifiable and individual data will always remain private unless you choose to share it. If there are large enough data groups to aggregate your data into without it there being a possibility of identifying you then we will do this so that we can show you comparisons of your data versus the average of a certain group - e.g. How do I compare to other developers in my city?

You can export your data from QuantifiedDev at any time if you want to use it elsewhere. You can also delete your data from QuantifiedDev at any time, though we hope you won't want to.
